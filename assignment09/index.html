Instructions

Step 1 - Use quality pie crust

  • Flour

  • Dont add too much water or your dough will be flimsy



Step 2 - Keep everything cold

Now that you havew your pie crust lets keep everything together. Keeping your pie dough as cold as possible is important because it helps prevent the fat from melting. If it melts the crust will lose shape in the oven.

Try out different designs!




Step 3 - Take your time and have fun!

take your time when working with pie dough. Be as creative as you want. Make a lattice or braid small strips together. Use cookie cutters to cut shapes out and layer on top for a beautiful look.
